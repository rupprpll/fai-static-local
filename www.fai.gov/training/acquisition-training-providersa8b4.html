Acquisition Training Providers

This page provides a list and links to commercial acquisition training providers. This list is not comprehensive or exclusive and it is meant to assist agencies with market research. FAI does not specifically endorse any of the vendors and has not reviewed training materials for alignment with FAC-competencies. If you are a commercial acquisition training provider and would like to be added to this list, you may contact the FAI help desk.

Note: Courses taken for Federal Acquisition Certification in Contracting (FAC-C) purposes must be taken from these DAU equivalent vendors.
